How I started;

She was born and raised in 1987 in Istanbul, Turkey. And currently lives and works in Denver,CO. Esra Baskan, who has a palette on one hand and a passion for creativity on the other, is a fine arts graduate. Her interest in painting at an early age turned her into an educator and artist today. She started at a prestigious art academy in Turkey, where she honed her skills in various mediums, from oil painting to sculpture. Driven to inspire and nurture the next generation of artists, She transitioned into teaching. As a fine arts teacher, she instills in her students the technical skills they need to succeed and the confidence to embrace their unique artistic visions.

Exhibitions;

The talented and passionate artist has exhibited his oil paintings, acrylic, and watercolor works in various media for eight years. She has participated in successful exhibitions in local galleries and prestigious art institutions in Turkey, Greece, Cyprus, and Denver, Colorado. She continues to push the boundaries of creativity and inspire others with the power of art. Present; Fine art prints of her work are available exclusively at Santa Fe Street.
